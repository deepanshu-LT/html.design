Q: What should I do if the clothing item I ordered doesn’t fit me correctly?
A: We understand the importance of finding the right fit. If your item doesn’t fit as expected, don’t worry! We offer hassle-free returns and exchanges within 30 days of purchase. Simply reach out to our customer service team, and we'll guide you through the return or exchange process, ensuring you get the perfect size for your comfort.


Q: How can I best care for my new clothing to maintain its quality?
A: Proper care ensures longevity for your favorite pieces. For most of our clothing items, we recommend following the care instructions on the label. Typically, a gentle machine wash in cold water and air drying or tumble drying on low heat helps maintain the fabric's quality. Avoiding harsh chemicals and bleaches also extends the life of your garments.


Q: I received my order, but one item seems to be missing. What should I do?
A: We apologize for any inconvenience caused by a missing item. Sometimes, orders might be shipped in separate packages. However, if you believe an item is genuinely missing, please contact our customer service team with your order number and details. We'll investigate the issue promptly and ensure the missing item is shipped to you without delay.


Q: Can I track my order once it’s been placed?
A: Yes, you can! Once your order is processed and shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. Use this number on our website's 'Track Order' section or on the courier's website to monitor the progress of your delivery.


Q: Do you offer international shipping?
A: Yes, we do offer international shipping to many countries. During the checkout process, you can select your country for delivery. Please note that shipping times and costs may vary depending on the destination country's regulations and shipping options chosen.
